Goal

In this game, you control a mom fish trying to get back to her eggs. She's so worried she keeps swimming forward, but you can help her to decide whether she must go UP, DOWN or stay STILL on the same height.

The game is played on a grid of 15 cells wide and 6 cells high. The cell on the top right corner's coordinate is (0, 0). Each turn, the fish goes one cell to the right. To succeed your mission, you must lead the fish to her eggs.
Initialization input
2 integers eggX and eggY representing the position of the group of eggs.
Input for a game round
2 integers fishX and fishY representing the position of the fish.
Output
One action among these:
- UP: Makes the fish go one cell higher.
- DOWN: Makes the fish go one cell lower.
- STILL: Does not change the fish's height.
Constraints
0 < eggX < 15
0 < eggY < 6
0 < fishX < 15
0 < fishY < 6
